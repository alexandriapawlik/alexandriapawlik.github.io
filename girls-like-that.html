Girls Like That

Design Concept

A suggestively realistic, contemporary preparatory high school.

​Through lighting techniques, I plan to display a realistic setting with small elements that are suggestive rather than realistic. 

Theme

An expose on the double standards, hypocrisy, and the false notions of the unification of women that re-occur throughout history.
Design Statement
Cuelist

Polar Capture Sketchup

Since this show was taken to a MIFA One-Act Competition, all materials needed to be portable. My plot depicts the individual pieces needed to set up my design. Additionally, actors were used as stage hands in setting up technical equipment for each performance. This meant directions were required for all actors without technical experience. These setup directions can be found in my "Setup" file.

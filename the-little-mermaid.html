The Little Mermaid

Design Concept

Theme
A colorful, animated underwater world. Realistic characteristics and creatures conveyed in a playful, unrealistic way, layered for depth. A fairytale palace modeled around architecture from 20th Century Britain. Realistic structures with whimsical elements alluding to the dream-like nature of life above the water.
A lighthearted story that emphasizes multiple lessons taught to children at a young age: don’t judge, don’t give up, don’t forget your roots. Centered on an audience of children and families. Suggests deeper themes of stereotyping and conformity.
